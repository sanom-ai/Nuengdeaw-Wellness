Nuengdeaw Wellness is a biometric wellness, biofeedback, and aura visualization platform designed for demonstration workflows, research-oriented exploration, future wearable-connected deployment, and commercial adaptation under a separate agreement.

## Research and License Status

- Free for personal thesis work, non-commercial academic study, teaching, and non-profit research use.
- Academic use must include attribution to `Nuengdeaw Wellness` and `TAWAN` in the thesis, paper, poster, presentation, report, or related output.
- Universities and educational institutions may request a special academic rate for internal installation used for teaching, laboratory work, or internal research.
- If a university, lab, department, or project uses the system to generate revenue, serve external clients, charge fees, or participate in a commercial collaboration, a commercial license is required.
- Research funded by a private company, involving a commercial partner, or intended for business exploitation is not treated as free academic use automatically and must be reviewed case by case.
- Public access to this repository does not grant commercial use rights.

## Why Nuengdeaw Wellness

- Distinctive `aura visualization` and `bio-aura` presentation layer.
- Supports `simulation`, `diagnostics`, `export`, `replay`, and `handoff workflow`.
- Ready for future `wearable integration` and organizational deployment.
- Includes a modern `wellness-v1_1` architecture path for future expansion.
- Suitable for demos, proof-of-concept work, academic exploration, and commercial adaptation under agreement.

## Use Cases

- Clinics or wellness centers that want visually guided biofeedback experiences.
- Wellness studios and performance support workflows.
- Research teams that need diagnostics, replay, and handoff-oriented workflows.
- Universities, teaching labs, and educational installations.
- Organizations exploring white-label wellness platforms.
