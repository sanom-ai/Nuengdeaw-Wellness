# Commercial License and Research Use Framework

This document outlines the intended use and licensing framework for Nuengdeaw Wellness. It is not a complete legal agreement. Commercial use requires a separate written agreement with TAWAN.

## 1. Free Academic and Thesis Use

Nuengdeaw Wellness may be used free of charge for the following non-commercial purposes:

- Personal thesis work
- Academic study
- Teaching and classroom demonstration
- Non-profit research
- Internal lab exploration without revenue generation

Conditions for free academic use:

- Attribution to `Nuengdeaw Wellness` and `TAWAN` is required in the thesis, paper, poster, presentation, report, or related output.
- No resale, sublicensing, commercial rebranding, or redistribution is allowed.
- The system may not be used to provide paid services to external clients under the free academic allowance.
- The user is responsible for ethics, consent, privacy, data handling, IRB or institutional review, and all applicable institutional or legal requirements.
- Research funded by a private entity, involving a commercial partner, or intended for business exploitation is not automatically covered by free academic use and must be reviewed case by case.

## 2. University Internal Installation at Special Rate

A university, faculty, laboratory, or educational institution may request a special academic rate for internal installation.

Typical eligible use cases:

- Teaching and coursework
- Internal university research
- Departmental or laboratory use
- Internal demonstrations and capability-building

Limitations of the special academic rate:

- The approved scope should be defined clearly, for example `1 lab`, `1 faculty`, `1 site`, or `1 department`.
- The special rate does not automatically include white-label rights.
- The special rate does not automatically allow redistribution of the source code to other units.
- The special rate does not cover revenue-generating services or paid external programs.

## 3. Revenue-Generating or Commercial Use

The following cases require a commercial license:

- Direct or indirect revenue generation using the system
- Paid services for external users or clients
- Fee-based wellness, clinic, training, consultation, or assessment workflows that rely on the system
- White-label, OEM, resale, or deployment for end customers
- University or educational deployments used in paid public programs, paid short courses, spin-offs, incubators, or external service operations
- Projects involving commercial partners or commercial exploitation

## 4. Attribution Requirement

For academic and free research use, users must provide appropriate attribution to:

- `Nuengdeaw Wellness`
- `TAWAN`

The attribution should appear in the thesis, paper, presentation, poster, report, repository, or other relevant academic output as appropriate.

## 5. Medical and Research Disclaimer

- Nuengdeaw Wellness is not a medical device.
- It must not be used as a substitute for diagnosis, treatment, or medical advice.
- Human-subject research use must comply with ethics review, consent, privacy, data governance, and institutional requirements.
- Users are responsible for regulatory and institutional compliance in their own jurisdiction and organization.

## 6. Public Repository Access

Public access to this repository does not mean that users receive:

- commercial use rights
- resale rights
- white-label rights
- deployment rights for paid services

Public access is intended for evaluation, study, academic exploration, and pre-engagement review only.
